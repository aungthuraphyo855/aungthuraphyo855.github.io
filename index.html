Biography

Aung Thu Ra Phyo (born 7 May 2006) is a Burmese actor and model. He is currently studying Mathematics at Mawlamyine University while pursuing his acting and modeling career. He was born in Paung, Paung Township, Mon State, and stands at 1.65 m tall.

He has appeared in various modeling and acting projects, gaining recognition for his presence and style. He continues striving to balance his academic life with his career in the entertainment industry, aiming to inspire Burmese youth to follow their passions.
