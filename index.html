About

Aung Thu Ra Phyo (born 7 May 2006) is a Burmese music producer and content creator. He is known for producing EDM and digital music content while building his presence across online platforms.

Alongside his studies in Mathematics at Mawlamyine University, he continues to develop his creative career, focusing on modern sound design and engaging content for a new generation of audiences.
